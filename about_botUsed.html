VIT Chennai

VIT University for the past 25 years has made a mark in the field of higher education in India imparting quality education in a cross-cultural ambience , intertwined with extensive application oriented research. Established by well-known educationalistand former Parliamentarian ,Dr. G Vishwanathan,Founder and Chandellor,is a visionary who transformed VIT into a center of excellence in higher technical education. His dream has taken the shape of VIT Chennai. Spearheaded by Mr.G.V.Selvam, Vice President, the institution is making leaps and bounds in professional programs close to the heart of Chennai City. The Vice President has a mission to make this a global center with the latest learning facilities. Dr. Anand A. Samuel, Pro-Vice Chancellor leads the team in Chennai with the following objectives:

  1. To maximize Industrial Connectivity.
  2. To create Centers of Excellence in niche areas ofresearch.
  3. To enrich Technological and Managerial Human Capital nurtured in a multicultural ambience.
  4. To provide a common platform for the agglomeration of ideas of personnel from various walks of life forlearning enrichment.
  5. To create opportunities and exploit the available resources to benefit industry /society.
  6. To encourage participation in the National Agenda of knowledge building.
  7. To foster International collaborations for mutual benefits in areas of research.
